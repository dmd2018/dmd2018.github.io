About

A message from Liam Birtles, Programme Leader for Digital Media Design -

It is a poignant and unique honour but also an absolute pleasure to be able to introduce the work of the final graduating cohort of BA Hons Digital Media Design at Bournemouth University in 2018.

Within this body of work, you will find some of the most innovative, critically framed and commercially focused creative digital media art and design practice that UK graduates in 2018 have to offer. From hybrid media forms that blend print and tag technologies through to new applications of binary decision-making processes, in this exhibition you will find an exciting range of forward looking work that pushes at the edges of digital media and that has an eye firmly on the future and its opportunities.

As always while the work finds insight and creative inspiration in an engagement with technological innovation and user experience, industrial placement provides a professional and commercial focus. The result is a challenging and pragmatic collection of work that demonstrates a capacity and preparedness to navigate a complex evolving media landscape creating useful, playful, pleasurable and socially-engaged digital media products and experiences.

So while great deal has change in the last 4 years and they digital media design graduates are emerging into an uncertain and complex business landscape, it is very clear from the presented work that they are prepared not only with an excellent skill-set but also with a clear sense what they hope to achieve in the future.

I look forward to watching their progress as they make their respective contributions to the field of Digital Media Design. I and the course teams wish them well in their careers.
